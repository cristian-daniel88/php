Details

This application is a tool for creating, amending, deleting and searching employee profiles within a company. It is designed to support connectivity between employees located within different branches and departments to foster communication across the company. With an easy to use search function, employees can locate their counterparts and wider personnel within the company quickly and efficiently, especially as search hits are listed alphabetically by name as a result of any search combinations.
